Tower rhythm

Water

4–6 gal every 2–3 days in summer. Finger-test the bottom row — if it's dry down there, soak; if moist, sip.

Worms

Feed the center column kitchen scraps as you cook. No citrus, onion, meat, or dairy. Harvest the tea drawer weekly and pour it back through the top.

Rotate

Give it a quarter-turn every day or two so no plant sulks. Vines trellised off the bottom will limit rotation late-season — that's normal.
